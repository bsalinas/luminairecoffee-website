Electrical Engineer

Location: Seattle (for the right candidate, Boston or Austin might be a possibility)

Our typical project goes a little bit like this: One of our partners will call us in for a meeting when there is a new product concept. From that point on, we will be involved in every step of turning that idea into a physical product. This involves

  • discussing design tradeoffs, technical requirements and user needs,
  • proposing and prototyping potential electrical solutions,
  • integrating and testing prototypes
  • designing the user experience in firmware
  • more testing of the implemented features
  • developing test equipment
  • overseeing the electronics manufacturing process

Our ideal candidate has experience with circuit design and PCB layout. On a daily basis, you would be interacting with our lead electrical engineer to design, layout, bring up, and troubleshoot circuit boards. Ultimately, we expect you to grow into a position where you will be able to own the full process of developing, prototyping, manufacturing, and supporting PCBs of your own design.

Day to Day Activities

On a typical day, you will do several of the following

  • Designing circuits in PCB layout software
  • Sitting in front of an espresso machine, bringing up circuit boards you have designed
  • Interpreting bugs from the field
  • Interacting with clients
  • Translating user needs into hardware requirements

Company Perks

  • Quarterly trips to Florence, Italy.
  • Flexible schedule
  • Part time options available
  • Ability to work on side projects
  • Equity in the company

To apply, e-mail careers@luminairecoffee.com with some details about why you are be a good fit.


Technician
