The Team

We've designed and built software, electronics, and medical devices used by millions of people around the world. Let us help you make your next product a success.

George Harris

Circuit Design Electronics Manufacturing Hardware-Software Interaction Advanced Control Systems

George, on a quest for perfection in all he does, brings the engineering industry's best practices to the circuits he designs and firmware he develops. He takes a data driven approach to engineering and product design and is a controls wizard. His work at Luminaire draws on experience at Microsoft, Bluefin Robotics, and as an engineer developing a self-driving car as part of the DARPA Grand Challenge. Ask George about prototyping electronics.

Andrew Coats

Mechanical Design Manufacturing Product Design Data Collection and Analysis

Andrew is a mechanical engineer with extensive experience designing and building medical devices with start-ups through Fortune 500 companies. Specializing in system design, Andrew focuses on the manufacturability and commercialization from the start. His work in medical products brings experience with unique technologies and manufacturing processes plus a focus on reliability and testing. Ask Andrew about high risk design.

Chester Macklin

Mechanical Design Product Design Circuit Design Software

Chester is a mechanical engineer who also designs his own circuit boards and writes software. Ben Salinas

Product Design Software Circuit Prototyping Hardware-Software Interaction
